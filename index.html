Services

Store Setups

From selecting the right theme, to implementing your design, I'll build out your store to match your vision so that you can focus on your business.

Store Migrations

Shopify is quickly becoming the #1 e-commerce platform thanks to it's ease of use and customizability. Have a store on Woocommerce, Wordpress, Wix, Squarespace or another site and want t convert into a Shopify store? I can help!

Technical SEO and Speed Optimization

The speed of your store determines how Gooogle ranks and indexes it. I will conduct a thorough analysis of your website's speed, identifying bottlenecks and areas for improvement. After this is complete I'll provide detailed, actionable recommendations for resolution.

Theme Customizations

As great as Shopify themes are, sometimes you may want a special customization like a button color change, a layout change or something more. My experience with Shopify's templating language, Liquid, qualifies me to customize your store code to tweak your site just the way you'd like it to look.

Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO)

Improve your store's User Experience to increase your conversion rates and ultimately your revenue. Request an audit and I'll evaluate each part of your store, identifying areas of friction and where users are likely dropping off.

App Integrations

⭐️ Using heat mapping software to optimize site ⭐️ Product subscription app set up and customization ⭐️ Setting up Shopify apps and integrations ⭐️ Support and training

Maintenance and Support

This is insurance for your website. If anything happens I will be ready to jump on it with instant downtime response. Includes monthly theme updates to make sure you stay up to date and nothing breaks your store. Starts at $99/mo

General Consultation

Just need to talk it out? With a general consultation, you'll be able to schedule a meeting to ask your questions, discuss possible solutions for site issues, and get feedback and resources.
