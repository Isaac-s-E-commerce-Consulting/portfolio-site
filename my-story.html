My Story

My goal is to enable eCommerce businesses to sell as much as possible online using Shopify.

Over the past 4 years, I have served over 30 clients across 15 industries.

I’ve helped them migrate from other platforms to Shopify, create brand new Shopify stores, customize themes to their liking, and increase their conversion rate through faster store speeds and more.

I’ve also worked with Shopify agencies as a project manager.

By focusing solely on Shopify I've gained insights and skills that make me uniquely equipped to tackle all the technical aspects of creating, customizing and managing a Shopify store.

I have been in the world of web development with Shopify since 2020 when I started Solcosm.com, a film education store and website. I know what it's like running a business and trying to manage a website at the same time. It can be a lot! That's where I come in. I love to help e-commerce store owners with all their technical needs so they can focus on their business. I look forward to speaking with you and would love to have a chat to see what exactly you're looking for.

A bit more about me...

I am based in Brooklyn but am often traveling to a new destination. In 2022 I was lucky enough to spend time in Mexico, Vietnam, Thailand, and Italy.

I have a bachelor's of science degree in Computer Engineering and Music from Lehigh University in addition to a Master's of Engineering in Technical Entrepreneurship.

I worked in the world of hardware at Adafruit Industries for 4 years as a Product Quality Engineer.

I have a deeply technical background however I enjoy creating art and combining technology with my artistic pursuits.

In my free time I like to travel, hike, ski, dance, sauna and help men overcome their barriers.
